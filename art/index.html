Everything about my artistic background

"Art, Undeniably, is conductive to happiness." Unknown.

I play the classical guitar since I was 13 years old. I studied different styles including Venezuelan waltz, tango, flamenco, baroque music, etc. In the recent years I have been rather inactive, however, I am playing whenever I have time.

Below, the Music Repertory I am preparing right now. I will be adding more and more guitar songs.
